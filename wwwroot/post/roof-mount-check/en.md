# Roof Mount Check After Wind

![Roof Mount Check After Wind](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Rooftop_solar_equipment.jpg)

The morning after high wind is for bolts, cable slack, enclosure seals, and the small lies told by yesterday's tidy installation photo.

Everything held except one cable loop that tapped the rail when the gusts shifted north. The fix was a shorter tie, not a new part.

Operational notes like this make the product journal feel maintained. Instruments live outside the mockup.
