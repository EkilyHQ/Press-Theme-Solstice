# Public Kiosk Copy Review

![Public Kiosk Copy Review](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Solar_kiosk_display.jpg)

The first kiosk draft said extreme ultraviolet. The second said light our eyes cannot see. The second kept more people reading.

Public science copy should not flatten the subject, but it should decide where the doorway is. A visitor can learn the instrument name after they understand why the image looks strange.

Approved copy: live solar image, updated every few minutes, colored to reveal structures beyond visible light.
