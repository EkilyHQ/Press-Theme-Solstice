# About Kai Lin Solar Lab

Kai Lin Solar Lab is a fictional product lab that designs interfaces for small observatories, classroom instruments, and public solar displays. The demo is written like a maintained product journal: release notes, experiment logs, display-mode decisions, alert copy reviews, and image-led technical essays. The lab, products, users, and metrics are invented for theme testing. NASA and public-domain solar imagery is cited in the articles so the demo can exercise media-heavy posts, source notes, and technical prose.

This demo uses the `solstice` theme and fictional editorial content generated for theme testing.
